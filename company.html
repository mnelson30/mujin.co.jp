Company History

2013 Apr MUJIN Inc. announced to go into partnership with CEC Inc., for development of new Service RoboDIA
2012 Oct MUJIN Inc. Opened new office at Koishikawa, Bunkyo. Koishikawa Office
2012 Sep MUJIN Inc. relocated its headquarters to Tokyo University Entrepreneur Plaza.
2012 Sep Industrial Robotics Software Startup MUJIN Inc. raises $950K Series A funding from UTEC -The University of Tokyo Edge Capital (UTEC Announcement)
2011 Jul MUJIN Inc. was founded in Tokyo, Japan. The team started to apply OpenRAVE technology to industrial robot system.
2008 Oct OpenRAVE started to be released as an open-source software.
2006 Carnegie Mellon University graduate student, Rosen Diankov, invented OpenRAVE, a robot manipulation planning platform that provides an environment for testing, developing, and deploying motion planning algorithms in real-world robotics applications."Go to OpenRAVE"

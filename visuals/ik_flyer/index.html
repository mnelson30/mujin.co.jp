Inverse Kinematics Solutions

MUJIN develops tools that can automatically solve the analytic inverse kinematics for complex kinematic structures. We can generate extremely robust and fast programs to find solutions that you can easily integrate into your product.


Our Technology

Traditionally, kinematic equations to solve the IK of a robot have been difficult and tedious to work with. Various numerical techniques exist to approximate analytic solutions, but they usually lack in speed or accuracy.

Here at MUJIN, we use general analytic IK solving algorithms to automatically solve inverse kinematics equations. MUJIN's framework supports any robot or kinematic structure, even if it involves more than 7 axes or closed kinematic chains.

Any Kinematic Structure

MUJIN is able to robustly solve the IK for robots with:

  • 7 or 8 DOF

  • redundant joints

  • closed kinematic chains

  • robots mounted on gantries or rails




Proven Solutions

MUJIN's IK Solver has a solid track record of analytically solving many different robot and kinematic structures.

Our solutions are used every day in real-world industrial applications.

Robust Program Generation

Our IK solutions are generated into programs that are robust, fast and extremely easy to integrate with other software. C++ and Python are natively supported. Other languages and platforms can call the IK solver externally.
